## 5. DOMAIN RECOMMENDATIONS

Given the actual brand (multi-venture founder, Cameroon-based, four companies under one identity), the studio name is the stronger long-term anchor — personal-name domains only work well once "Zayne" alone is a recognized identity, and right now the companies are doing the identity work.

**Priority order:**
1. `ajtechcompany.com` or `ajtech.company` — matches the real registered entity name, most defensible long-term
2. `ajtechlabs.com` — if the full "AJ Tech Company Ltd" is too long for casual use
3. A personal domain **only if** you want a founder-brand site separate from the company sites — in that case, use your actual full name rather than "zayne" alone, since single-word first-name domains are harder to rank and easy to confuse with others

I'd hold off recommending `.ai` domains speculatively — they're expensive to renew and only worth it if AJ Tech's positioning is explicitly AI-product-led, which isn't clearly the case yet from what's been built (EataLyft's AI piece is one microservice, not the core product).
